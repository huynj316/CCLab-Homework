How it works

Designers

Step 1: Sign up as a designer and create your profile! Indicate your areas of expertise (web, graphic, and/or branding), talk about yourself, and link your personal portfolios.

Step 2: Choose areas of interests to curate a list of non-profits you want to help out. Filter based on:

  • budget vs. pro-bono work
  • on-site vs. remote
  • time-frame vs. open-ended

Step 3: Once you find a project that you're interested in, click "I'm interested!" and the non-profit will be notified. If you guys have a mutual interest, you can start stacking up your volunteer hours!

Non-Profits

Step 1: Sign up as a non-profit and create your profile! Explain the goals of your Non-Profit, include links to projects you're proud of, and include where your company is based.

Step 2: Create a new listing. Get help in web, graphic, and/or branding design. Indicate whether you would like:

  • budget vs. pro-bono work
  • on-site vs. remote
  • time-frame vs. open-ended

Step 3: Now, instead of playing a waiting game, you will have a curated feed of all the designers we think meet your needs. You can click to let them know you're interested. They'll get a notification, and if they agree, it's a match made in Enhance!
